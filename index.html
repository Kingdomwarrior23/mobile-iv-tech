Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session

How long does an IV therapy session take?

Most sessions take between 30 and 75 minutes depending on the treatment. Hydration and energy drips are typically faster (30–45 min), while treatments like NAD+ or Myers' Cocktail may take 60–75 minutes.

Is IV therapy safe?

Yes. All treatments are administered by a licensed nurse using sterile, medical-grade equipment. We follow strict safety protocols and conduct a brief health screening before each session to make sure IV therapy is right for you.

How quickly will I feel results?

Most clients report feeling better within 1–2 hours of their session. Energy and hydration benefits are typically felt the same day, while immune support benefits build over the following days.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Lubbock, TX and surrounding areas. We come to your home, office, hotel, Airbnb, or event venue. If you're unsure whether your location is in our service area, just give us a call.

Do you offer group or event packages?

Yes! We provide IV therapy for events, bachelor/bachelorette parties, corporate wellness days, athletic events, and more. Contact us for group pricing and availability — we can handle groups of any size.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online through our scheduling page or call us directly at (806) 790-4455. Same-day appointments are often available. We accept all major credit cards.
